Frequently Asked Questions

What is BudgetVision App?
BudgetVision is an Android Application which keeps track of your personal expenses to determine the best daily budget for you !

Is BudgetVision App free ?
Yes, the app is free for everyone.

What are the features of BudgetVision App?
  • Keep track of all your personal expenses.
  • Keep track of expenses you owe to other individuals.
  • Sort all your personal expenses and the expenses you owe to others numerically or alphabetically
  • All personal expenses can be read aloud via text-to-speech
  • Export all your expenses to a google sheet in your Google Account.
  • Convert between currencies from default CAD to one of the top 25 most traded currencies in the world.
